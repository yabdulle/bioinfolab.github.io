Recent Work

Featured Publication

Integrative multi-omics analysis identifies novel molecular signatures in ALS pathogenesis

Nature Neuroscience, 2024

We performed comprehensive transcriptomic, proteomic, and metabolomic profiling of spinal cord tissue from 500+ ALS patients and controls. Using network-based integration methods, we identified three previously uncharacterized molecular modules associated with disease progression and validated their functional relevance through experimental perturbation studies.
